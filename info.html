The Wedding & Reception

We will be married on Saturday, July 13, 2013 at 1:30pm at Our Lady of Grace in Greensburg, PA. Afterwards, a reception for the couple will be held at 5pm at Ferrante's Lakeview. Cold beverages, home-made cookies, a tasty dinner, and good music await you. Bring your dancing shoes.

Destinations

Greensburg has many attractions that you can explore while in town for our wedding. We've include a few of our favorites.

  • Westmoreland Mall - 5256 State Route 30
  • The Cellar Door Cafe & Creperie - 114 North Main Street
  • Target, Lowes, Giant Eagle - 6206 State Rte 30
  • Westmoreland Museum of American Art - 221 North Main Street
  • Smokey Bones - 100 Power Line Drive (beside the reception hall)

Lodging

We have reserved several rooms at The Courtyard by Marriott in Greensburg. This reservation rewards guests with a discounted rate. Simply call the hotel and mention the McCutcheon-Sherman wedding. The Courtyard is directly behind the reception hall. They offer a shuttle to and from the church and the reception hall. When you check in, be sure to tell them when you need access to the shuttle.

Directions to the church

From Beaver Falls

  1. Take I-376 East toward Pittsburgh
  2. Take exit 78A to merge onto U.S. 30 East toward Forest Hills
  3. Continue on U.S. 30 East for 23 miles
  4. Take the Greensburg/Mt Pleasant exit
  5. Turn right onto Mt. Pleasant Road
  6. Take the first left onto Pellis Road
  7. Arrive at Our Lady of Grace (1001 Mount Pleasant Road)

From Indiana

  1. Take U.S. 22 West towards Blairsville/Pittsburgh
  2. After 12.5 miles, turn left onto US-119 South
  3. Turn left onto State Route 1053
  4. Turn right onto Bovard Luxor Road
  5. Quickly, make a slight left onto Georges Station Road (State Route 1053)
  6. Turn right onto U.S. 30 West
  7. Take the exit toward Mt. Pleasant Road (Greesburg/Mt. Pleasant)
  8. Take the first left onto Pellis Road
  9. Arrive at Our Lady of Grace (1001 Mount Pleasant Road)
